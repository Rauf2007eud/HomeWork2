1 - Wheat tortilla recipe

Composition

  • Wheat four - 200 g;
  • Warm water - 70 ml;
  • Salt - 1/2 teaspoon;
  • Vegetable oil - 1 tablespoon;

Preparation

  • 1.Sift flour on the table with a slide, make a small depression in the middle.
  • 2.Dissolve salt in water.
  • 3.Pour water into the recess, add oil.
  • 4.Mix with careful movements, knead the dough.
  • 5.Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  • 6.From the dough, roll out thin tortillas about 25-30 cm in diameter.
  • 7.Fry in a dry hot frying pan for 10-15 seconds on each side.

  • 5.Dip fillets in eggs, roll in flour, dip in eggs, roll in flakes.
  • 6.Put it in s sacepan or put it on a preheated frying pan. Fry until golden brown.

    • Preparation

Chef Burger

Ingridients

  • 1.Chiken fillet - 100 g;
  • 2.Salt, and garlic;
  • 3.Corn flakes - 30 g;
  • 4.Flour - 30 g;
  • 5.Eggs - 2 pcs;
  • 6.Vegetable oil.

Preparation

  • 1.Wash the chicken meat, remove the skin cut into medium pieces, add salt and spices cover with cling film and leave for a couple of hours.
  • 2.Grind corn flakes, beat eggs, sift Flour into a separate plate.
  • 3.Dip a piece of fillet in eggs, roll in flour then again in eggs and at the end sprinkle well with flakes.
  • 4.Put it in a frying pan with preheated oil or in a deep-frying pan. Fry until fully cooked.

Twister

Ingridients

  • 1.Wheat flour - 300 g;
  • 2.Butter - 100 g;
  • 3.Warm water - 150 g;
  • 4.Salt - 1 tsp;
  • 5.Baking powder - 1 tsp;
  • 6.Hard cheese - 150 g;

Preparation

  • 1.Sift flour, mix with baking powder and salt, add melted butter.
  • 2.Add grated cheese, pour in water , mix, knead the dough.
  • 3.Cover the dough with cling film and leave for 10 - 20 minutes.
  • 4.Divide the dough into several parts, roll out into thin cakes, fry on both sides in a dry frying pan with a thick bottom.
